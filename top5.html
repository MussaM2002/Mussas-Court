My Top 5 NBA Players of All Time

1. LeBron James

A 4-time NBA champion, an all-time leading scorer, and an unmatched all-around player.

2. Michael Jordan

The GOAT. 6-time NBA champion, 5-time MVP, and a player who defined greatness.

3. Kobe Bryant

The Black Mamba. His killer mentality and 5 rings make him legendary.

4. Kareem Abdul-Jabbar

All-time scoring leader before LeBron, legendary skyhook shot.

5. Stephen Curry

Revolutionized basketball with his deep three-point shooting and 4 NBA championships.
